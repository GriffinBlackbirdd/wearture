Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

7-Day Return Window
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags and packaging.

How to Return

1
Contact Us

Message us on Instagram @wearxture or email us at support@wearxture.com with your order number.

2
Wait for Confirmation

Our team will review your request and provide return instructions within 24 hours.

3
Ship Your Return

Pack items in original packaging and send to the address provided. We'll process your refund within 5-7 days after receipt.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We offer exchanges within 7 days of delivery, subject to availability. Follow the same process as returns but specify that you want an exchange instead of a refund.
